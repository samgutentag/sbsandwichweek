How is this calculated? ▼

Score = (Directions + Phone) × 3 + (Direct Links + Shares + Likes) × 2 + (Website + Instagram + Views) × 1

3× Directions & Phone — strongest visit intent, someone is heading there or calling

2× Direct Links, Shares & Likes — active engagement, someone shared a link or upvoted a favorite

1× Website, Instagram & Views — casual browsing and interest

Contact = Website + Phone + Instagram combined
